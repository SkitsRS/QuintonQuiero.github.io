About Me


I am a Pennsylvania based self taught multi-media designer, videographer, and photographer with a passion for making creative works. I sparked an interest in creation at an early age when I discovered the joy it brought to others. I am always striving to learn new things and create a positive difference in the world, whether that be brought through social change on a national scale, or through inspiring one person at a time. I have a bachelor's degree in Interactive Media, from the school of Harrisburg University of Science and Technology.
